Nike Air Max 95 Smoke Grey

The Nike Air Max 95 QS ‘Japan’ recognizes the country’s influence on American style and culture. The upper is crafted from overlapping layers of textile, highlighted by varying weaves and a gradient color scheme that fades from black to grey to white on the side panels. Webbing eyelets are rendered in Chile Red, while the reflective grey heel panel features a tonal cherry blossom print. The sneaker is mounted on a solid white polyurethane midsole with red-tinged Air-sole units in the forefoot and heel. Underfoot, the rubber outsole is enlivened with the cherry blossom pattern in red and white.
